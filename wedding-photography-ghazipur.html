Ghazipur's Trusted Wedding Photographer

Your Wedding, Our Passion

Shyam Studio is a professional wedding photography studio based in Jalalabad, Ghazipur, serving families across the Ghazipur district and eastern Uttar Pradesh for over 20 years. What started as a small family studio in 2003 has grown into one of the most trusted names in wedding photography in the region.

We understand the soul of Indian weddings — the sacred rituals of the Haldi, the vibrant energy of the Sangeet, the emotional Vidaai, and the joyful celebration of the Baraat. Whether your wedding is a grand affair in the heart of Ghazipur city or an intimate ceremony in a nearby village, we bring the same dedication, artistry, and professionalism to every celebration.

From Jalalabad to Saidpur, from Mohammadabad to Zamania — families trust Shyam Studio to preserve their most precious memories with cinematic quality and heartfelt storytelling. We don't just take photographs; we capture the emotions, the traditions, and the love that make your wedding truly yours.
